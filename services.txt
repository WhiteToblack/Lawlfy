Services Breakdown

Accounting: Handles billing, payments, invoices, etc.
AI Integrator: Manages AI-powered case predictions, document analysis, etc.
Analytics: Provides data insights and reports.
Authentication/Authorization: Manages user authentication and access control.
Campaign: Manages marketing campaigns and client notifications.
Document Manager: Handles the storage, retrieval, and version control of legal documents.
Firm Manager: Manages law firm data, including users, cases, and clients.
Log: Centralized logging service.
Notification: Handles notifications (email, SMS, push notifications).
OCR: Optical Character Recognition for scanning and processing legal documents.
Task Manager: Handles automated reminders and task management for users.
